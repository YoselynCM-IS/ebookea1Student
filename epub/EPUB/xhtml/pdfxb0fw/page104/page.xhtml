Complex Sentences

 

A complex sentence is a sentence that is made up of one independent clause and one or more

 

dependent clauses.

 

To make a delicious pasta,  
you need to follow the instructions carefully 
.

 

Dependent clause Independent clause

 

Independent clauses have a subject and a simple predicate and can stand alone as a sentence.

 

You need to follow the instructions carefully.

 

Subject:  
Yo u

 

Simple predicate:  
need to follow the instructions carefully. 
(This predicate is simple because it only

 

has one main verb with auxiliary.)

 

A dependent clause is a group of words with a subject and a verb. It does not express a complete

 

thought so it is not a sentence and can't stand alone.

 

To make a delicious pasta 
, (It stands alone because it does not express a complete thought, it has

 

no sense, it feels incomplete.)
