Hi, Thomas! I love my new house! My sister and I have a big bedroom. Everything is pink

 

and purple! There is big window and there’s a small closet next to my sister’s bed. We

 

have clothes and shoes in it. There is a long rug between the beds. There is a night table,

 

with a drawer and a lamp on it. And under the drawer I have my photo album. There is a

 

mirror on the wall. There isn’t a TV in my room. There are big flowers on the wall, above

 

my bed. There are cushions on the beds and on the floor. There aren’t books in my room,

 

because I don’t have a bookshelf. There are beds under the beds! Come to visit me soon!

 

Jessie
