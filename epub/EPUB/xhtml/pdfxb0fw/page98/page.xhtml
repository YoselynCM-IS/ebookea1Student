Compound sentences

 

A compound sentence is a sentence that has two clauses joined by a coma, semicolon,

 

conjunctions, or linkers.

 

Carla and Alex are cleaning the house, but they need to finish their homework too.

 

Alondra is planning a party; she has to think carefully.

 

The children received a surprise, they are happy.

 

Wendy cleans the table and Sandy washes the dishes.
