Simple sentences

 

A simple sentence has one main clause, with no dependent or subordinate clauses.

 

The elements in a simple sentence are subject and predicate.

 

Laura works in a hospital.

 

S P

 

There are two types of simple sentences.

 

• Compound Verbs: Sentences with one subject and two verbs.

 

The children are playing and screaming.

 

• Single Subject and a Single Verb: Sentences with one subject and one verb.

 

Rosa is writing a letter.
